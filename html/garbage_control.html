Garbage Control Policy

  • The unit owner or tenant shall use disposable garbage plastics or recycled shopping bag to contain garbage from his unit until it is ready for disposal into the MRF.
    • Garbage must be segregated inside the unit
    • Dry garbage (non-food and other non-biodegradable materials) should be securely wrapped or enclosed in the black plastic garbage bag.
    • Dust and powdered waste must be wrapped in compact packages and placed inside the garbage bag.
    • Wet garbage (food and other bio-degradable materials) shall be thoroughly drained from solid wastes to liquid wastes and separately wrapped in plastic and placed in the garbage bag and put it in the garbage drum provided inside. Keep the drum closed at all times.
    • Loose papers shall be crushed into tight bundles.
    • Disposing of bottles, glassware, sharp or heavy object and hazardous waste into the garbage chute is strictly not allowed as these items may damage the garbage drum, just place these items on the floor inside the MRF.
    • The burning of garbage or any other waste material inside the unit or in any portion of the building is absolutely prohibited.
    • Any item constituting a fire hazard such as cigarettes or matches must be properly extinguished and placed in appropriate containers.
    • Garbage, refuse and waste should not be left outside the unit, inside the fire exit area or in any place of the building. It should be properly disposed of in the MRF.
    • Units shall be kept clean at all times and in tenable condition. The beating of rugs and/or shaking of dust cloth are prohibited in the unit main door, hallways, balcony, outside of the windows of the unit and promontory. Throwing or sweeping of dirt or any other material out of or from the unit main door, windows, balcony, windows passages or areas of common use is strictly not allowed.
